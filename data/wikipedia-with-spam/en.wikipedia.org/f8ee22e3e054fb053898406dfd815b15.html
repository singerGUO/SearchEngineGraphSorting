Music[edit]

  • Seattle (album), a 1969 album by Perry Como
  • "Seattle" (song), a 1968 song composed by Hugo Montenegro, later covered by Perry Como, title track to the above
  • "Seattle", a 1987 single by Public Image Limited from their album Happy?

Ships[edit]

  • MS Seattle, a German cargo ship that came under crossfire and sank off Kristiansand, Norway during World War II
  • USS Seattle (ACR-11), a Tennessee-class armored cruiser launched in 1905 as Washington, renamed in 1916
  • USS Seattle (AOE-3), a Sacramento-class fast combat support ship launched in 1968

Other uses[edit]

  • Seattle Redhawks, the above university's athletic program
