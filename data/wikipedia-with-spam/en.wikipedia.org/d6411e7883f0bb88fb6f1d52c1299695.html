Magnolia Beach, Washington

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Magnolia Beach is an unincorporated community in King County, in the U.S. state of Washington.[1]

History[edit]

A post office called Magnolia Beach was established in 1908, and remained in operation until 1953.[2] The community's name in part is a transfer from Magnolia, Iowa, the former home of a family of first settlers.[3]
