Summary[edit]

DescriptionReagans with Michael Jackson.jpg

President Ronald Reagan presents Michael Jackson with an award for his work against drunk driving at a White House ceremony in 1984. First Lady Nancy Reagan is at right.
Date 14 May 1984
Source [1]
Author White House photo by Jack Kightlinger
Permission
(Reusing this file)

Photo caption notes the author as Jack Kightlinger; Kightlinger was a White House photographer for the Johnson, Nixon, Ford, Carter, and Reagan administrations.[2] As a photo taken by Kightlinger between 1981 and 1989, it is a White House photo and is in the public domain.
