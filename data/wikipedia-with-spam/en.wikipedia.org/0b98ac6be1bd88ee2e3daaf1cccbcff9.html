List of reservations[edit]

Official Name Pop. Area
(acres)		 Location of Reservation
Confederated Tribes of the Chehalis Reservation 833 4,438 Southeastern Grays Harbor County and southwestern Thurston County.
Colville Indian Reservation 7,587 2,116,802 Primarily in the southeastern section of Okanogan County and the southern half of Ferry County.
Cowlitz Reservation -- 152 Near La Center, in northern Clark County
Hoh Indian Reservation 102 477 The Pacific Coast of Jefferson County.
Jamestown S'Klallam Indian Reservation 594 13.49 Near Sequim Bay, in extreme eastern Clallam County.
Kalispel Indian Reservation ~400 4,557 The town of Cusick, in Pend Oreille County.
Lower Elwha Indian Reservation 776 -- The mouth of the Elwha River, in Clallam County.
Lummi Indian Reservation 6,590 13,437 West of Bellingham, in western Whatcom County.
Makah Indian Reservation 1,356 30,011 On Cape Flattery in Clallam County.
Muckleshoot Indian Reservation ~3,300 3,921.92 Southeast of Auburn in King County.
Nisqually Indian Reservation 588 1,728 Western Pierce County and eastern Thurston County.
Nooksack Indian Reservation 1,800 2,720 Town of Deming, Washington in western Whatcom County.
Port Gamble Indian Reservation 1,234 1,303 Port Gamble Bay in Clallam County.
Port Madison Reservation (Suquamish Indian Reservation) -- -- Western and northern shores of Port Madison, northern Kitsap County.
Puyallup Indian Reservation ~ 4,000 18,270 Primarily northern Pierce County.
Quileute Indian Reservation 371 1,003.4 Southwestern portion of the Olympic Peninsula in Clallam County.
Quinault Indian Nation 2,535 208,150 Primarily the north coast of Grays Harbor County.
Samish Indian Reservation 1,835 79 (Samish also owns another 130+/- acres of non-trust land) Anacortes.
Sauk-Suiattle Indian Reservation ~200 107 Near Darrington in southern Skagit County.
Shoalwater Bay Indian Reservation 70 665.4 Along Willapa Bay in northwestern Pacific County.
Skokomish Indian Reservation 796 -- Just north of Shelton in Mason County.
Snoqualmie Indian Reservation ~650 -- Snoqualmie Valley in east King and Snohomish Counties.
Spokane Indian Reservation 2,708 152.011 Almost entirely in southern Stevens County and in northeastern Lincoln County along the Spokane River.
Squaxin Island Indian Reservation 936 1,715.46 The entirely of Squaxin Island and the town of Kamilche in Mason County.
Stillaguamish Indian Reservation 237 -- Along the Stillaguamish River in Snohomish County.
Swinomish Indian Reservation 778 10,350 The southeastern side of Fidalgo Island in Skagit County.
Tulalip Indian Reservation 2,500-2,800 22,567 Port Susan in western Snohomish County.
Upper Skagit Indian Reservation ~200 110.45 Western Skagit County near the towns of Sedro-Woolley and Burlington.
Yakama Indian Reservation 10,851 1,399,000 Primarily in southern Yakima County and in the northern edge of Klickitat County.
