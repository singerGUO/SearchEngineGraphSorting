List of cover versions of Michael Jackson songs

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

This is a list of recording music artists who have covered one or more songs originally recorded by American pop singer Michael Jackson. A cover is a new performance or recording of a previously recorded, commercially released song.

Artists who have covered songs from The Jackson 5 career are not included.

This is a dynamic list and may never be able to satisfy particular standards for completeness. You can help by expanding it with reliably sourced entries.
