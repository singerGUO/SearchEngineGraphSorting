Michael Jackson was an American singer and entertainer, his cultural significance means there are a large number of articles covering or involving him. All related articles about Jackson are/should be linked here, along with any media (including song samples and photos), while articles on tours, songs and albums are/should be linked to in their relevant separate sub-categories.

To add articles to this category or its sub-categories, please see Wikipedia:Category.
