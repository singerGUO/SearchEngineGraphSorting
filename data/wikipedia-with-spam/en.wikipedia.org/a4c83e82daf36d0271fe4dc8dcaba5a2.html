The Michael Jackson Mix is a compilation album by American recording artist Michael Jackson, released in 1987. It contains 40 songs from Jackson's career - solo and with The Jackson 5 - edited together in four separate megamixes; Love Mix 1, Love Mix 2, Dance Mix 1 and Dance Mix 2.
