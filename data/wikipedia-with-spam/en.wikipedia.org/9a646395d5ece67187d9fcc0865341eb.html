Statistics[edit]

In 2008, there were 268,624 crimes reported in Washington, including 193 murders.[1] In 2014, there were 281,842 crimes reported, including 174 murders.[1]

Capital punishment laws[edit]

Main article: Capital punishment in Washington state

Capital punishment is applied in this state.[2]
