Anthony Ralston (born 16 November 1998) is a Scottish professional footballer who plays for Celtic in the Scottish Premiership. He also spent several months on loan at Queens Park in 2015. [1][2][3]

Career[edit]

Ralston made his debut for the Celtic first team on 11 May 2016, in a 2–1 defeat against St Johnstone.[4] He scored his first goal for Celtic in a 5–0 Scottish League Cup win over Kilmarnock on 8 August 2017.[5]
