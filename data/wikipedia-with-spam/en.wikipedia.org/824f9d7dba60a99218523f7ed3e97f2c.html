The Seattle Gay News is a weekly newspaper aimed at the Seattle, and Puget Sound area LGBT community.

SGN files are preserved in the Washington State History Museum in Tacoma, Washington.

Editor: George Bakan; Associate Editor: Shaun Knittel; Staff Writers: Mike Andrew, James Whitely

Circulation[edit]

13,500 Weekly (54,000 - 60,000 monthly), 800 Subscribers (3,200 monthly), 14,000 Weekly internet readers (56,000 monthly), With a readership of 32,500 weekly, (130,000 monthly) [1]
