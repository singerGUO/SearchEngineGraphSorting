Summary[edit]

DescriptionSpace Needle 360 Panorama.jpg
English: 360° Panoramic view from Space Needle, Seattle, WA. Pictures taken from the top, seamless all around. The panorama starts with Elliott Bay of Puget Sound on the left and then rotates clockwise, showing Queen Anne, Lake Union, I-5, Downtown and then again Elliott Bay with some cranes from Seattle Harbor. This is a higher-quality remake of a previous image of mine.
Date 29 April 2010
