Technology company

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

A technology company (often tech company) is a type of business entity that focuses primarily on the development and manufacturing of technology.

Apple, IBM, Lenovo, Huawei, Microsoft, and Oracle, among others, are considered prototypical technology companies.[1]

Information technology (IT) companies and high tech companies are subsets of technology companies.
