The band member history of American R&B and pop group The Jackson 5 shows how the band has comprised different members of the Jackson family at various times during its chronology, with differing names as The Jackson Brothers (1964–1965), The Jackson 5 (1965–1975, with Motown) and The Jacksons (1975–1989, with CBS).

Members[edit]

Jackie Jackson
Active: 1964–1990, 2001, 2012–present
Instrument: vocals, tambourine, percussion
Tito Jackson
Active: 1964–1990, 2001, 2012–present
Instruments: vocals, lead guitar, bass guitar, rhythm guitar, electric guitar
Jermaine Jackson
Active: 1964–1975, 1983–1990, 2001, 2012–present
Instruments: vocals, rhythm guitar, bass guitar
Marlon Jackson
Active: 1964–1985, 2001, 2012–present
Instruments: vocals, conga, tambourine, percussion
Michael Jackson
Active: 1964–1984, 2001
Instruments: lead vocals, conga, tambourine
Randy Jackson
Active: 1975–1990, 2001
Instruments: percussion, keyboards, vocals
