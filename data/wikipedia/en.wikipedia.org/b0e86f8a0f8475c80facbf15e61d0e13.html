Bobby Brooks
Birth name Robert Green Brooks
Born (1957-08-19) August 19, 1957 (age 60)
Minneapolis, Minnesota, United States
Genres R&B, heavy metal, hip hop, hard rock, alternative rock, country, rapcore, techno, pop, jazz
Occupation(s) Record producer, Audio engineer
Years active 1977–present
Labels Universal/Interscope, Atlantic, Warner Bros., Epic

Robert Green "Bobby" Brooks (born August 19, 1957 in Minneapolis, Minnesota) is an American record producer and audio engineer. He is given credit for his signature R&B sound as well as shaping Motown's hits.[citation needed]
