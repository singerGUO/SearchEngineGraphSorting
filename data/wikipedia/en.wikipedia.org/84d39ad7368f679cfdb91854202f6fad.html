Republican Party of American Samoa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Republican Party of American Samoa is the affiliate of the U.S. Republican Party in American Samoa. The party was founded by Peter Tali Coleman.[1]

Current Leadership[edit]

In March 2016, the party elected new leadership in the lead up to the 2016 American Samoa Republican Caucus.[2]

Party Leader Position
Utu Abe Malae Chairman
John Raynar Vice-Chairman
Aumua Amata Coleman Radewagen National Committeewoman
Su'a Carl Schuster National Committeeman
Sailitafa Samoa Secretary
Tina Ione Treasurer

[2]

In the 2014 elections, Radewagen was elected as American Samoa's Delegate to Congress.[3]
