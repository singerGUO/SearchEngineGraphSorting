The Alaska-Yukon-Pacific Exposition on the campus of the University of Washington in Seattle, Washington in 1909. In the background is Mount Rainier. Cropped from the original at the UW Libraries Special Collection: http://www.lib.washington.edu/specialcoll/collections/ayp/image6.html


Originally published as a postcard that was released concurrently with the A-Y-P Exposition in 1909.[1]
