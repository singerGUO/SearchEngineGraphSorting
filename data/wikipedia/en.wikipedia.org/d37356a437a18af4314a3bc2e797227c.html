The Whittier Daily News is a paid local daily newspaper for Whittier, California. Coverage area includes Whittier, South Whittier, Pico Rivera, La Habra Heights, Santa Fe Springs and La Mirada. The Whittier Daily News is a member of Southern California News Group[1] (formerly the Los Angeles Newspaper Group), a division of Digital First Media. It is also part of the San Gabriel Valley Newspaper Group, along with the San Gabriel Valley Tribune and the Pasadena Star-News. After nearly 120 years of operating at its Whittier offices, Whittier Daily News moved its operations from Whittier to Monrovia on August 8, 2016, one year after the San Gabriel Valley Tribune moved its operations from West Covina to Monrovia.

Thomson Newspapers purchased the paper in 1982. Thomson sold the Daily News to William Dean Singleton's MediaNews Group in 1996.
