DescriptionKing County Washington Incorporated and Unincorporated areas Seattle Highlighted.svg

This map shows the incorporated and unincorporated areas in King County, Washington, highlighting Seattle in red. It was created with a custom script with US Census Bureau data and modified with Inkscape.
